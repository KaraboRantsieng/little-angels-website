Vision & Mission

Our Vision is to bring the community together through children and learning, while laying a strong foundation to prepare the kids for grade 12 and primary school We want our daycare centre to be a place trusted by the community, being a home away from home for the children

Our mission is to reach our goal will open our hearts with love, give love, be patient and wear our brightest smiles We will make the safety for our children and community a top priority and our almost best to offer learning through play.
